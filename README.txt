A plugin to automatically generate and manage WooCommerce discount coupons for customers based on their purchase categories.

== Description ==

Coupon Mailer is a WooCommerce extension designed to dynamically generate and manage discount coupons for your customers based on the categories of products they purchase. This plugin helps you reward your customers with discounts for their next purchases, thereby increasing customer loyalty and sales. The customer gets emailed the coupon together with the order email so that they can see the coupon.

**Features:**

* Generate discount coupons dynamically based on product categories after an order is placed.
* Manage featured images for product categories.
* Delete used and expired coupons automatically.
* Limit coupon usage to specific users.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `coupon-mailer` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the settings in WooCommerce > Settings > Coupon Mailer.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I configure the plugin? =

Navigate to WooCommerce > Settings > Coupon Mailer to configure the plugin settings.

= How are the coupons generated? =

Coupons are generated dynamically after a customer places an order without using a coupon. The generated coupons are based on the categories of products purchased.

= Can I limit coupons to specific users? =

Yes, you can limit the usage of coupons to specific users by enabling the relevant setting in the plugin configuration.
